Our Approach

Growth, on
our own terms.

Every new neighbourhood we enter is a deliberate choice. We don't expand for the sake of scale — we expand when we find a community that wants what we build.

That means fewer locations, opened more slowly, designed with more care. By 2026, three cafes across Southwest Florida. That's it. That's the plan.
